About us

Why should you consider a career with us? What makes Hot Bean Web the right place for you?

Who we are

We design and build websites with purpose. Every choice is made with the user and goal in mind, whether that’s improving accessibility, simplifying content, or just making things look and feel better. The focus is always on clean layouts, smooth performance, and consistent branding that works across all devices.

What we do

We’re a small, focused team that cares about doing things properly. We pay attention to detail, stay flexible, and keep the bigger picture in mind. Our work combines technical skill and creative thinking, and we enjoy finding simple, effective solutions through design and development.

Why us

We create thoughtful, effective designs that are clear, accessible, and consistent. 94% of first impressions are design-related, and visitors form opinions in just 50 milliseconds. We focus on functionality as much as appearance because 75% of visitors trust well-designed websites more. We work within real limits to deliver strong, reliable results that help your brand connect with users.

Our Process

Our process is simple and straightforward. We start by understanding what’s needed, then plan and design before moving into development. We test throughout, check everything across devices, and stay open to feedback. Once it’s launched, we’re still around to help keep things running smoothly.
